About Me

Building what matters

I'm a Product Owner with 6+ years of experience turning regulatory complexity into elegant user experiences across EdTech SaaS, Cloud infrastructure, and FinTech. I hold an MBA from IIT Kharagpur and a B.Tech from MNNIT Allahabad, and I've led end-to-end product work on systems that directly affect how schools report data to governments and how enterprises manage cloud subscriptions.

At PowerSchool I own a $9M ARR compliance product used by 80+ school districts. I spearheaded the architectural shift from legacy XML to real-time Ed-Fi API standards — a platform-level transformation that improved data integrity, cut integration time for new clients, and positioned the product for a new generation of state-mandated data standards.

I've also contributed to Digital payment research at the Reserve Bank of India, analysing emerging CBDC architectures and experience strengthened my ability to evaluate complex technologies, balance innovation with trust, and apply an analytical mindset to product strategy.
