About Me

Building products that matter

I'm a Senior Product Manager with 6+ years of experience turning regulatory complexity into elegant user experiences across EdTech SaaS, Cloud infrastructure, and FinTech. I hold an MBA from IIT Kharagpur and a B.Tech from MNNIT Allahabad, and I've led end-to-end product work on systems that directly affect how schools report data to governments and how enterprises manage cloud subscriptions.

At PowerSchool I own a $9M ARR compliance platform used by 80+ school districts. I spearheaded the architectural shift from legacy XML to real-time Ed-Fi API standards — a platform-level transformation that improved data integrity, cut integration time for new clients, and positioned the product for a new generation of state-mandated data standards.

I've also done original AI & payments research at the Reserve Bank of India, analysing CBDC architectures and ML-driven fraud detection models at a time when India's digital currency strategy was being formed. That background informs how I think about AI product decisions today.

I want to grow into a product leadership role where I can set product direction, mentor PMs, and build AI-native products that solve genuinely hard problems for users.
